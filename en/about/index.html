The Editoria11y accessibility checker is trusted by more than 40,000 websites to meet editors where they are:

  • More than 80 tests run in real-time, offering inline corrections and advice as authors type.
  • Tips use plain language that both correct and teach.
  • Turnkey CMS plugins "just work;" they are installed in-site, not in-browser, so editors automatically see the results, even if they have never come to a training.

Editoria11y plugins integrate deeply into popular content management systems, allowing for as-you-type checking and creating site-wide dashboards where results can be reviewed and managed:

  • Drupal
  • WordPress
  • Kirby (contrib)
  • CraftCMS (contrib)

Editoria11y project members drive development, documentation & support.

  • The community contributes through developer time, sponsored work or add-on subscriptions.
  • Discussions guide which feature requests are selected for development.
  • Members find support in the forums and issue queues.
