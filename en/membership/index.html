Membership

Editor's Ally members guide & support the development of the Editoria11y library and its CMS plugins.

Members also have access to the "Community Supported Add-ons" (CSA) suite for Drupal and WordPress (eta mid-2026):

CSA Plugins

  • Developer and contrast tests
  • "Split configuration" for separate rulesets for developers and content creators.
  • Site-wide issue dismissals
  • Dashboard maintenance tools and crawlers
  • Future features from the project roadmap

CSA benefits

  • Priority support and over-weighted voting in issue queues and on project Slack channels.
  • Ability to test and influence in-development features.
  • (Optional) public supporter credit.
  • Direct assistance* with plugin installation and configuration.

* Everyone gets equal help on Slack and the issue queues. Those contributing more than $200/year, can request to credit some of their support towards consulting hours rather than project development.

Pathways to membership

Membership is tracked via subscriptions to CSA plugins, but not all subscriptions have an annual cost.

  • Monthly/annual per-site subscriptions.
  • Unlimited-site enterprise subscriptions.
  • Subscription credits from grants or directly sponsored work.
  • Subscription credits for approved contributions in Editoria11y issue queues.
  • Subscription credits for approved contributions to notably improve the accessibility of key open source projects.

Subscriptions list at $10/site/month as of 2026, with various discounts listed on the plugin licensing pages. Expect the list price to scale with inflation. If future features require cloud APIs we will talk as a community whether to bundle those hosting costs or offer them as an optional add-on.

Project maintainers consider granting contribution credits based on effort and impact. Please seek pre-approval if you are working under the assumption of approval, or plan to request credit for more than a 12-month site credit.
